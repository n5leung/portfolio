✦ Poster Design ✦

Title: Where Are You At? Where Do You Want to Go?

Project type: Poster Design

Tools: Adobe Express, Generative AI

⟡ Description ⟡

This project explores my evolving identity as a designer through a cohesive series of AI-generated posters. Using Adobe Express and Firefly, I created four 11"×17" vertical portraits that communicate my design philosophy, creative principles, personal style, and long-term goals. Each poster translates abstract ideas—such as taste, values, and aesthetic preferences—into bold visuals that represent who I am as a digital creator.

The series reflects my interest in blending human intention with AI tools, using generative design not as a shortcut but as a medium for experimentation. Through color, composition, and visual storytelling, these posters present a clear narrative of my identity as a designer today and the direction I want to grow toward.

⟡ Reflection ⟡

1. Concept & Intent

This project tent to visualize my own identity as a designer through a series of AI-generated portraits. The posters are created as a way to express my taste, values, and creative direction. Each piece focuses on a different aspect of my design identity—my philosophy, principles, goals, and aesthetic style.

2. Process & Use of AI

I used Adobe Express’s AI image generation feature to blend a photo of myself with a comic-inspired style, creating visuals that aligned closely with my artistic direction. Even though the visuals were generated by AI, the direction, creative process, and final design decisions were human-driven. This project taught me how to use GenAI thoughtfully—as a tool to enhance idealization rather than replace the design process.

3. Design Decisions

To create a cohesive series, I focused on consistent color palettes, typography, and layout structures. My choices reflect my preference for clean visual hierarchy, balanced composition, and expressive yet minimalistic storytelling. Each poster connects back to a core part of my identity and what I value in design.

4. Key Learnings

Through this project, I gained a clearer understanding of my own visual style and how to communicate it effectively. I also learned how to integrate generative AI into a professional workflow while maintaining creative control. This series helped me reflect who I am as a designer today, and where I hope to grow in the future.
